How Custom Fields Work

Custom fields let you add your own categories and labels to items in the archive. For example, you can tag dance works with a Genre like Ballet, Modern, or Duncan.

Step-by-step: Creating a new custom field
  1. Click "Add Custom Field" above.
  2. Enter a name (e.g., "Genre"). The field type will default to "choice" (dropdown).
  3. Check the boxes for which types of items this field applies to (e.g., Dance Work).
  4. In the Choices box, enter your options — one per line (e.g., Ballet, Modern, Duncan).
  5. Click Save.
  6. Now go edit a Dance Work — you'll see a Genre dropdown at the bottom of the page!
